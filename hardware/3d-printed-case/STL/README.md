## üñ®Ô∏è Printing Instructions

### Black Parts (Main Enclosure)
- **Material:** [PolyMaker Matte Black PLA](https://amzn.to/4atrUPS) (recommended) or any black PLA
- **Finish:** Matte preferred for professional look
- **Parts:** Base, cover, mounting parts

### White Parts (LED Lens)
- **Material:** [PolyMaker Matte White PLA](https://amzn.to/4qquieL) - **STRONGLY RECOMMENDED**
- **Finish:** **Matte finish is CRITICAL** for proper light diffusion
- **Why Matte White:**
  - Optimal optical reflection properties
  - Even light distribution from RGB LED
  - Professional frosted appearance
  - Glossy white will create hot spots and uneven lighting

### Print Settings (All Parts)
- **Layer Height:** 0.2mm (standard quality)
- **Infill:** 20% (sufficient strength)
- **Supports:** May be required depending on geometry
- **Build Plate Adhesion:** Brim or raft recommended for small parts
